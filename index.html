About Me

Salt & Sovereignty grew out of my own journey through trauma, therapy, witchcraft, and devotion to Hekate. I do not claim to have all the answers. I share what I am learning as I walk this path, including the doubts, the breakthroughs, the messy middle, and the quiet moments of magic that remind me I am not alone.

This space is for witchcraft, shadow work, spiritual awakening, trauma-informed healing, and the slow practice of reclaiming your power. Whether I am recording an episode, writing The Lost Goddess Trilogy, or offering coaching, my work circles the same truth: healing is not about becoming someone new. Sometimes it is about finally staying with the parts of yourself you had to leave behind.

The Lost Goddess Trilogy

A trauma-healing podcaster and an immortal Dragon King become entangled in an ancient divine conflict when emotional magic begins awakening across the world. As forgotten gods, dragons, and buried identities resurface, they must discover whether love can exist without possession and whether freedom is worth the cost of becoming fully known.
