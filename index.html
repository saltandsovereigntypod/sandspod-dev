About Me

Hi, I'm Ashley, the creator of Salt & Sovereignty, host of the Salt & Sovereignty podcast, and author of the upcoming Lost Goddess Trilogy.

My work lives at the intersection of spirituality, storytelling, and healing. As a Hekatean witch, I believe that our relationship with the divine is deeply personal, shaped as much by our lived experiences as by the traditions that came before us. Through my podcast, writing, and the Salt & Sovereignty platform, my hope is to create spaces where people feel safe to explore their spirituality with honesty, curiosity, and a sense of belonging.

Whether I'm recording a podcast episode, building digital tools that make spiritual practice more accessible, or writing fantasy inspired by devotion, mythology, and transformation, everything I create is guided by the same belief: healing isn't about becoming someone else. It's about reclaiming the parts of yourself that have always deserved to be seen, heard, and loved.

Podcast

Salt & Sovereignty

Salt & Sovereignty is a podcast exploring witchcraft, spirituality, trauma-informed healing, and the lifelong journey of becoming more fully yourself. Through conversations about Hekate, shadow work, devotion, folklore, ritual, and personal growth, each episode invites listeners to approach spirituality with curiosity, discernment, and compassion rather than fear or perfection.

This isn't a podcast about having all the answers. It's about asking better questions. Whether you're an experienced practitioner, quietly exploring your spirituality for the first time, or simply searching for a more authentic way of moving through the world, you'll find thoughtful conversations that encourage you to honor both tradition and your own lived experience. The goal has never been to tell you what to believe. It's to help you discover the truth that keeps you sovereign.
