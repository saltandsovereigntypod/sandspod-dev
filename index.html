Meet Ashley

A storyteller at the altar of becoming.

Ashley is the creator behind Salt & Sovereignty, a space for the sacred and the practical: moonlit reflection, honest growth, ancestral curiosity, ritual craft, and the everyday choices that return a person to their own authority.

Her work gathers spirituality, witchcraft, mythology, romantic fantasy, and personal sovereignty into stories that feel ancient and immediate. Whether she is recording an episode, drafting a mythological retelling, or guiding a client through a creative threshold, Ashley is interested in the places where magic becomes lived wisdom.
